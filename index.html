Methodological Framework

Our comprehensive approach to analyzing urban service accessibility

Population Origins

Estimation of population residence centroids and demographic distribution analysis.

Local Service Units

Identification of Local Units with "proximity" economic activity through detailed ATECO classification.

Service Reachability

Estimation of travel times for reaching neighborhood services within 15 minutes.

Service Indexes

Estimation of microterritorial indicators aggregatable by hierarchical geographical levels.

Data Processing

Integration of ISTAT resident data, business units, and geographical information.

Territorial Analysis

Advanced mapping and spatial analysis of service distribution patterns.
