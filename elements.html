Detailed Methodology

Input Data

Our analysis relies on comprehensive data sources:

  • ISTAT Residents: Population by age groups, nationality, gender, family composition at census section level
  • ISTAT Local Units: Structure (5-digit ATECO, legal form, employees), Location (address, municipality, ZIP code), Performance metrics
  • ATECO Classification: Sections, divisions, groups, classes, categories and subcategories
  • Shapefiles: Administrative boundaries and Structural Aggregates

Service Accessibility Analysis

The project processes:

  • 1.8 million centroids distributing 59 million residents (2021)
  • 2.3 million georeferenced local units offering proximity services
  • 282 million street distances in travel times between 0 and 15 minutes

Service Indexes

We analyze service accessibility through multiple metrics:

  • Travel times by census section to reach the nearest service point
  • Number of service points reachable within 15 minutes walking distance
  • Gross and net gravitating residents within 15-minute radius of each Service Point
  • Exclusivity ratio between net and gross gravitating residents
